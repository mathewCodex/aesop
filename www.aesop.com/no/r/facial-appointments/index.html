    Facial Appointments

    Composed skin, at hand

    Meticulous attention to detail, impeccable technique, intense nourishment and exquisite touch: such are the hallmarks of Aesop Facial Appointments.

    Offered at select locations around the globe, our facials are designed to nurture both the skin and senses. From the moment you enter the aromatic hush of your facial suite, your expertly trained facial therapist is entirely devoted to ensuring you enjoy an enriching experience, for optimal results.

    Beginning with a consultation to understand your skin, your appointment will be tailored to address your individual requirements. Facials also include custom-blended formulations to care for specific skin needs.

    Post-facial, your facial therapist will provide you with a suggested Aesop skin care regimen for continued pleasure and replenishment at home.

    Facials offered

    All facials are tailored to suit your skin type. It is recommended you enjoy an Aesop Facial Appointment every four to six weeks, as well as before travel and significant events, and with each change of the season.

    Anti-oxidants

    Our facials employ replenishing, anti-oxidant-rich formulations to help fortify skin that is subjected to the strains of city living, as well as artificial heating and cooling. Frequently selected ingredients include Vitamin C and Vitamin E, as well as certain botanical extracts renowned for their skin-supportive properties, such as Parsley Seed, Green Tea and Blackcurrant Seed.

    Exfoliants

    Particularly suited to mature skin, chemical exfoliants work to bring balance—helping to address congestion as well as even the skin’s surface and appearance. Select formulations containing Lactic Acid provide gentle yet efficient exfoliation, assisting with natural skin cell turnover to effect softer, smoother, more clarified skin. Where needed, a physical exfoliant may also be used for a tailored and more robust approach.

    Vitamins

    Our range of skin care formulations features a number of vitamins, including Vitamins B, C, D, E and F. Each ingredient is chosen for its ability to calm, balance, nourish or fortify the skin, and address conditions such as dryness, dehydration or depletion.

    Extraction

    To address congestion resulting from the day-to-day strains of city living, our trained facial therapists perform extractions—using only their hands—to help clear pores of any build-up of dead skin, excess oil and other debris.

    Massage

    Massage is a fundamental part of the facial experience. Depending on the length of your appointment, you may receive a massage of the face, neckline, arms, hands and scalp to assist with circulation and bring about a state of relaxation.
