    For the bathing connoisseur

    Those well-practised in the art of washing may hone their craft further by exploring different formats that cater to a variety of skin needs and aromatic preferences.

    Responsibly sourced

    Nurture, Polish and Refresh Bar Soaps are produced using RSPO-certified sustainable (segregated) Palm Oil. Though it may seem that the best solution is to replace palm oil altogether, when farmed sustainably, this type is a more efficient crop compared to other vegetable oils. Each Bar Soap is also housed in a recyclable, FSC-certified carton.
