    A live video consultation

    We are pleased to now offer video appointments with trained Aesop consultants, conducted in the comfort of your home. Each consultation is tailored to your unique needs and could include recommendations on gift-giving, your skin care regimen, or guidance through our range of aromatic formulations for body and home, with consultants conducting product demonstrations, sharing application techniques and providing personalised advice.

    Reservations are complimentary and are for a duration of 30 minutes, allowing ample time to discuss the topic of your choosing. Consultations are available in English, Cantonese and Chinese Mandarin, and all are conducted live via video on your desktop, tablet or mobile device. A 4G or WIFI connection is required.
